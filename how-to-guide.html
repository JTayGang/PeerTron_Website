How to Undervolt Your Hardware

Follow these steps to safely undervolt your CPU and GPU for better performance and efficiency.

Example of MSI Afterburner, one of many tools you can use for undervolting.

Step 1: Choose the Right Software

Use a tool like MSI Afterburner for GPUs to adjust voltage settings.

Step 2: Test Stability

Run stress tests (e.g., Prime95, FurMark) to ensure your system remains stable after undervolting.

Step 3: Monitor Results

Use monitoring tools like HWMonitor or GPU-Z to track temperature and performance changes.
