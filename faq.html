Frequently Asked Questions

Is undervolting safe?

Yes, undervolting is generally safe as long as you test for stability and avoid extreme settings.

Will undervolting void my warranty?

No, undervolting does not void warranties since it does not involve physical modifications and has no risk of damaging the card.

Can I undervolt any CPU or GPU?

Most modern CPUs and GPUs support undervolting, but results may vary depending on the hardware. (Some Intel chips may have the feature locked for example)
