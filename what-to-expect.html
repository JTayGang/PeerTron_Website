What to Expect When Undervolting

Undervolting can bring significant benefits, but it’s important to know what to expect before diving in.

Example of performance improvement after undervolt. (Better sustained boost clock with lower temperatures)

Benefits

  • Lower Temperatures: Your hardware will run cooler, reducing thermal throttling.
  • Improved Efficiency: Lower power consumption means longer battery life for laptops.
  • Quieter Operation: Fans won’t need to spin as fast, reducing noise.

Challenges you WILL encounter

  • Stability Testing: You’ll need to test for stability to avoid crashes.
  • Time-Consuming: Finding the optimal voltage can take time.
  • Technical Knowledge: Some understanding of hardware is required.
