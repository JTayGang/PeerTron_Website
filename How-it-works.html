How PeerTron Works

PeerTron makes workplace transparency simple. Here's the breakdown:

  • Anonymous Submissions: Users securely submit salary, benefits, and work experience data.
  • Verification Checks: We apply automated and manual review methods to filter out spam and fake entries.
  • Clear Presentation: Data is grouped by position, company, location, and time period to show trends.
  • Privacy First: No personal identifiers are collected or stored — ever.
